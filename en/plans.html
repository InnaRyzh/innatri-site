Good to know

Questions, answered.

Is this an app I download from the App Store?

No store, no waiting. After purchase you get a link and a key — open it in your browser and tap "Add to Home Screen." It installs like an app, opens full-screen, and works completely offline. We'll send simple step-by-step instructions (with pictures) in your welcome email.

Does it work on both iPhone and Android?

Yes. It runs on any modern phone — iPhone, Android, even your laptop. Your progress syncs to a private cloud backup, so if you switch phones, your data comes with you.

I'm a total beginner. Is this too advanced?

The 5K plan is built for exactly you — it starts with a gentle run/walk progression and never assumes you're already fit. Every hard workout has a beginner modification. You set your starting point in a 2-minute setup and the plan meets you there.

Can I train around my own schedule?

Absolutely. You choose which days you run, and the plan reshapes each week around them. Miss a day? Nothing breaks — the app simply keeps you on track without the guilt.

Is it a subscription?

Never. You pay once and the plan is yours to keep and repeat as many times as you like. No recurring charges, no surprises.

What if it's not for me?

You're covered by a 14-day money-back guarantee. Train with it, and if it's not the right fit, email me for a full refund — no hard feelings.
